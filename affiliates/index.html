Team Fabricio Philippines

Affiliated Schools

Over 30 gyms across the Philippines and beyond — all part of the Team Fabricio international BJJ family.

KMA Fitness & Martial Arts is the Philippine headquarters of Team Fabricio, the international BJJ network founded by Master Fabricio Martins. Under Professor Stephen Kamphuis's leadership, the network has grown to cover Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao — and even has an international affiliate in New Zealand.
