About Estie

Competitor and coach

Estie Liwanen is a BJJ black belt and active competitor who represents KMA and Team Fabricio Philippines on the international stage. Most recently, she competed at the Open Masters Games in Abu Dhabi 2026, bringing home a medal for the Philippines.

At KMA, Estie teaches both Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and Judo. Her competition experience informs her coaching style, bringing real-world fight knowledge to every session.

Whether you're training for competition or learning fundamentals, Estie brings focused instruction that helps students of all levels improve their ground game and throwing technique.
