About Ken

Competitor turned coach

Ken Menia is a BJJ competitor and instructor at KMA. A gold medalist at the Philippine Jiu Jitsu Federation Selection Trials, Ken brings real competition experience to his teaching.

At KMA, Ken focuses on BJJ instruction — helping students of all levels sharpen their technique on the mats. His competition background means he understands what works under pressure, and he brings that intensity and precision to every class.

Whether you're drilling fundamentals or preparing for your next tournament, Ken's approach is hands-on and practical.
